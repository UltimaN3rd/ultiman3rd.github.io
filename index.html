Steal Godot

Free & open source (Godot)
June 28th 2016
Sokoban puzzle game

The Bevy of AAA Developers have shut down every free game development engine. It's up to you to break into their facility, figure out the puzzles and steal the Godot game engine back! A short puzzle game featuring blocks, switches, doors and ice. Play in-browser or download on GameJolt
